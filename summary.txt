Here's a simple summary of the auditor appointment filing for you:

Alupa Foods Private Limited, based in Udupi, Karnataka, has appointed Mallya & Mallya, a firm headed by a partner with membership number 001955S, as their auditor.
The appointment is for a period of 5 years, from April 1, 2022, to March 31, 2027, which is within the legal limits.
The appointment was made on the AGM date of September 26, 2022, and no joint auditors were appointed.
The auditor rotation rules do not apply in this case.
The auditor firm has previously served the company for 6 years, from April 1, 2016, to March 31, 2022.
The company has submitted several documents as supporting evidence, including consent and resolution letters, an intimation letter, and an acceptance letter.
