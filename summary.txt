Alupa Foods Private Limited, based in Udupi, Karnataka, has appointed Mallya & Mallya as their auditor.
The appointment is for a period of five years, from April 1, 2022, to March 31, 2027, and was made on the Annual General Meeting (AGM) date of September 26, 2022.
No joint auditors have been appointed, and the appointment is within the legal limits.
The company is not subject to auditor rotation rules.
The necessary supporting documents, including the consent and resolution for the appointment, have been submitted and are available for review.
